We are standing at the threshold of the greatest paradigm shift since the horse and carriage. For over a century, the heartbeat of the automobile was the internal combustion engine. Today, that heartbeat is being replaced by the hum of electrons.

What is lost in sound is gained in instantaneous torque. What is lost in mechanical complexity is gained in aerodynamic freedom. APEX explores how manufacturers are struggling to maintain "soul" when the symphony of exhaust notes has been silenced.
